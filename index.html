What We Do

Our Focus Areas

Where scientific rigor meets practical implementation.

🧬

Nanoparticle Drug Delivery

Guidance on designing liposomes, polymeric nanoparticles, and lipid-based carriers to enhance efficacy and reduce toxicity. Our approach covers particle size, surface charge, drug loading, and controlled release — supporting scalable, regulatory-compliant nanomedicines for oncology and chronic diseases.

⚠️

Nanoparticles & VOC Health

Risk analysis, exposure studies, and mitigation for safer environments. We integrate real-time monitoring of airborne nanoparticles alongside VOC sensors to detect species like formaldehyde, benzene, and toluene — deploying advanced filtration to protect workers, patients, and researchers.

🏭

Biopharma Contamination Control

Science-led contamination control addressing microbial, particulate, and cross-contamination risks. Aligned with cGMP, EU GMP Annex 1, and ISO 14644 standards — ensuring aseptic environments from cell culture to fill-finish operations.

🏥

Surgical Emissions

Safer operating rooms through evidence-based smoke and aerosol mitigation. We provide advanced filtration and ventilation strategies to capture harmful airborne contaminants at the source, protecting both healthcare workers and patients.

🌬️

IVF Air Quality

Optimized laboratory environments for better IVF outcomes. Real-time particle and VOC monitoring with HEPA, ULPA, and activated carbon filtration — integrated with pressurized positive airflow and laminar flow workstations validated to ISO 5 and ESHRE standards.

About Us

Founded by Dr. Lakshmi Yeruva

SciTatva was founded by Dr. Lakshmi Yeruva, bringing together deep scientific expertise and a shared vision of translating advanced research into meaningful real-world impact.

Dr. Samrajya Lakshmi Yeruva is an accomplished researcher and biotechnologist specializing in bio-nanotechnology, targeted drug delivery, and reproductive biology. She is widely recognized for pioneering work in bio-inspired nanomedicine — particularly the development of lactoferrin-based nanoparticles for the safe and effective delivery of antiretroviral (ARV) drugs and microbicides.

During her doctoral research at the University of Hyderabad, she developed advanced nanoformulations that significantly enhanced the bioavailability, biodistribution, and pharmacokinetic performance of HIV/AIDS therapies such as Zidovudine (AZT), while substantially reducing toxic side effects without compromising antiviral efficacy.

Her scientific contributions include highly cited peer-reviewed publications in leading journals such as Scientific Reports and Pharmaceutical Research, with a strong focus on innovative combination therapies and translational solutions to combat complex viral diseases.
